Lexicon:

(Noun) The vocabulary of a person, language, or branch of knowledge. a.k.a what the hell are we talking about?

Cooking is a passionate pursuit, as such it should come from the heart. The writing on A Food Odyssey reflects this but as a result of wanting to sound authentic, it may contain certain obscure references and in-jokes that may be unfamiliar. They are not to be taken seriously, they are intended to be a bit of fun. As you will find, most of them are influenced by our other, non-cookery related hobbies. These references are highlighted within the articles, but the lexicon describes them in more detail for those brave enough to delve a little deeper.
